### \\( T(n) = aT(n/b) + O(n^k) \\)

* Bottom - heavy: \\( a > b^k \\)
  - \\(T(n)\\) = # leaf nodes = \\(O(a^D)\\) = \\(O(n^{loga / logb})\\) 
* Top - heavy: \\(a < b^k \\)
  - \\(T(n)\\) = \\(O(n^k)\\)
* Steady state: \\(a = b^k\\)
  - \\(T(n)\\) = \\( O(n^k log\_bn) \\)
